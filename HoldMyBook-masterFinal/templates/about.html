Welcome to E-Library:OLMS
What we really do?

E-Library:OLMS is a web application which provides the platform to a librarian to control all the daily activities related to the books of a library of an organisation. Are these activities limited to the transactions of physical books? No, users can buy and sell e-copies of books on this system. Borrowing and lending of books is also possible with the use of this web application.

Our Vision

One of our goals is to make the management process easy for the librarian and to assist them to carry out the all the activities efficiently which can save their time.

Our Vision

We would also like to help the users of an organisation to be able to carry out exchanges of books and maybe clear out their stock of last year books. Also, the e-books era is here, isn't it?
